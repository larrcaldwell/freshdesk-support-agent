# truDigital Support Playbook — distilled from resolved tickets (June–July 2026)

This is how the truDigital support team actually resolves the most common ticket
types, based on real closed tickets. Match these patterns when drafting replies.

## Voice & style

- Open with "Hello {first name}," and "Thanks for reaching out!"
- Short, warm, action-first sentences. Confirm what was DONE, not what will be considered.
- Close with "Please let me know if you need any other assistance!"
- Support contact info: O: (801) 852-9898 · E: support@trudigital.net · W: www.trudigital.com
- Customer login portal: app.trudigital.net

## Common requests and how the team resolves them

### Add / remove a user (very common — 10+ per month)
- Support adds or removes users directly; the customer cannot always self-serve.
- Ask for: the player serial number(s) and/or the playlist name(s) the user needs access to, plus the new user's name and email.
- New users receive a temporary password by email and log in at app.trudigital.net.
- Example reply: "I was able to get {name} added for you. They will get a temporary password sent to them."

### Change account email
- Support changes it; a new temporary password is automatically sent because of the change. Direct them to app.trudigital.net to log in.

### Player offline / not reporting
- Check the account first — if the player shows offline, send the "Forgetting and Reconnecting WiFi" KB article. Most customers fix it themselves from that.

### Device won't power on / black screen (hardware)
1. Check if the player is under warranty.
2. Troubleshoot BEFORE issuing a replacement: different outlet? different power cord? plugged into a surge protector?
3. If troubleshooting fails and warranty applies, send the RMA Authorization form.
4. RMA terms the customer agrees to: $150 fee if the defective device isn't returned within 30 days; $60 re-stocking fee if the fault is found to be the customer's (bad WiFi, faulty TV, faulty power source); $15 per missing accessory (power adapter, HDMI cable, remote).
5. After the form is submitted, ship the replacement and send the UPS tracking number.

### Wrong/erroneous message on screen due to billing state
- If a suspension/billing message appeared on a paying customer's screen in error: apologize, fix the player, and credit a month of service for the downtime. Content resumes within ~10 minutes.

### Turn off player-offline email notifications
- Support can disable these notifications on request (e.g., site closed temporarily).

### YouTube videos / content questions
- Offer to explain how YouTube integration and video files work; happy to hop on a Teams/Zoom call the customer schedules.

### Pricing, quotes, plan changes, cancellations
- Do NOT quote prices in support. Book the customer with their Account Manager (AM). Cancellation and upgrade requests also route to the AM/billing team.

### Escalation notes
- Backend user-creation issues (e.g., Stytch errors) escalate to R&D.
- Billing disputes, statements, invoices: handled by truDigital Billing Team (billing@trudigital.com), not support.

## What NOT to auto-handle
- Payment remittance notices, collections threads, "WON DEAL!" sales handoffs,
  voicemail/call-log tickets, and internal order-fulfillment tickets are workflow
  noise — triage only, never draft customer replies for these.
